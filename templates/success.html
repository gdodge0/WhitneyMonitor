Success - Sign in to Continue

Follow these quick steps to access our group's recreation.gov account with the provided credentials and confirm the permit waiting for you in your cart. You have 15 minutes to finalize the checkout process.

1

Go to Recreation.gov

Open your browser and navigate to recreation.gov. On desktop, the Sign In button sits in the upper‑right corner. On mobile, open the hamburger menu to locate it.

2

Enter Credentials

In the sign‑in modal, enter your username and password then click Sign In. If prompted to update your password or verify your email, you can safely skip or postpone these steps.

3

Open Your Cart

Once logged in, look for the cart icon — again, it's at the top‑right on desktop (or inside the mobile menu). Click or tap the icon to open your cart.

4

Review the Item Waiting for You

You should see at least one reservation or permit already in your cart. Double‑check the details, and when you're ready, click Proceed to Checkout or Complete Reservation to finalize.
